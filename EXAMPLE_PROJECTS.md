## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific with Skills**: Use exact technology names (e.g., "React" instead of "frontend")
2. **Balance Team Size and Budget**: Consider $70K-$100K per person annually
3. **Match Complexity to Experience**: More complex projects need higher experience levels
4. **Include Soft Skills**: Don't forget interpersonal skills that make teams successful
5. **Consider Duration**: Longer projects may require more experienced team members

## Expected Outcomes

The AI will:
- Match team members based on all criteria
- Ensure role diversity (avoid too many of the same role)
- Optimize for both skill match and budget
- Provide coverage analysis showing how well requirements are met
- Give detailed reasoning for each team member selection
- Calculate total cost and overall match score
